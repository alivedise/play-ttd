PlayTTD: The browser game based upon on Transport Tycoon Deluxe

PlayTTD are business simulation game, presented in an isometric view in 2D. Player is in control of a transport company, and can compete against rival companies to make as much profit as possible by transporting passengers and various goods by road, rail, sea and air. The game is awesome!
